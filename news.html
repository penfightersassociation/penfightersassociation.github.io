News


RIO GEL RETURNS!

Rio Gel, a sensational Luxor ballpoint pen returns in September, with a new upgrade, after two years of lying under several layers of trash.


RIO Gel was cast aside as a weakling because it losts its refill in 2017. Thanks to its neodymium ball magnets, RIO Gel is back and stronger than ever!



UNIBALL JETSTREAM IS BACK!

Uniball Jetstream, a full-grip defense pen from 2018, has undergone an upgrade in September and has become even stronger!


The Neodymium ball magnets that have been packed into this pen have complemented its strong defense, making it even more invincible!



RIO Gel Crashes!

RIO Gel, a recently upgraded 2017 grip pen has undergone an injury on September 23 2019. The rear part of the pen to which the stopper is attached came off in the middle of a match, spewing out its neodymium ball magnets. Thankfully, none were lost. Fortunately shortly after the match, the pen was repaired using superglue, and is now back in the arena!
