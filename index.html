Welcome to
PFA is an initiative founded by the students of APL Global School to promote Pen Fight as an engaging way of time pass as well as a strategic sport. In this site, you can learn the hacks and tricks to be the better player, and all the fancy bells and whistles that you can use to enhance your pen's strength. Learn the different kinds of pens, different brands, and the best to use. Also learn about the long history of the strongest pens. So, let's play pen fight!
