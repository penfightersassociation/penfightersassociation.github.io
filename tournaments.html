Ongoing Tournaments


The first tournament of PFA is scheduled to start sometime in the first week of October. Admissions are open.


Rules of Season 1


In this season, every player will play exactly one match against each other in every round.

Win: Gain 2 points
Lose: Don't gain any points
Sudden Death: 1 point for each player

At the end of a round, after all matches have gotten over, the 3 players with the least points will be eliminated. The rest will move on to the next round. The number of players eliminated can vary based on the number of players left. The last player standing is the champion.

Points get reset at the start of a new round. If you are playing with a borrowed pen, you start of with -5 points. The pens cannot have external modifications and upgrades in this season, but may be permitted in future seasons.

1st place: 100 credits
2nd place: 75 credits
3rd place: 50 credits
Use your credits to purchase pen modifications to enhance your pen's strength!

Good Luck!
